What grants can I apply for to upgrade my building's energy systems?
Advisor
Several schemes apply. For SMEs, the Energy Efficiency Grant (EEG) from Enterprise Singapore covers up to 70% of qualifying costs for pre-approved energy-efficient equipment. For larger industrials, EMA's Energy Efficiency Fund (E2F) supports audits and retrofits. If you're refinancing the upgrade, BREEF provides green loans at preferential rates.
