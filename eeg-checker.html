Step 1 of 5
What sector does your business operate in?
The EEG currently covers 6 specific sectors. Base Tier is expanding to all sectors from late 2026 under the April 2026 Ministerial Statement.
Step 2 of 5
Is your business registered and operating in Singapore?
Your company must be registered with ACRA and actively operating in Singapore at the time of application.
Step 3 of 5
What is your group annual sales turnover?
This is the combined turnover of your company and all related entities in your corporate group. This determines both eligibility and your support rate.
Step 4 of 5
What is your local shareholding percentage?
Most EEG sectors require a minimum of 30% local shareholding. Data centres are the only sector exempt from this requirement.
Step 5 of 5
Has the equipment purchase or project already started?
EEG requires that the project has not commenced at the point of application. Equipment ordered or installed before applying cannot be claimed. Note: you may purchase equipment after submitting your application but before receiving the outcome — at your own risk.

About the EEG

Scheme timeline

Extended to 31 March 2027. Base Tier expanding to all sectors and extended to 31 March 2028 per April 2026 Ministerial Statement.

Two tiers of support

Base Tier covers pre-approved equipment up to S$30,000. Advanced Tier (selected sectors) supports larger investments up to S$350,000 combined.

Qualifying equipment

Pre-approved energy-efficient equipment listed on GoBusiness. Advanced Tier requires demonstrated savings above 350 tonnes lifetime carbon abatement.

Claim window

Once approved, you have up to 1 year to purchase, install, and submit your claim for reimbursement.
